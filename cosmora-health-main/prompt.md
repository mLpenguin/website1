Cosmora uses AI to detect diseases like lung cancer from a simple retinal photograph.

There is a well-established connection between retinal microvasculature and systemic disease. Emerging research supports retinal biomarkers linked to lung cancer and other internal conditions.

**The insight:**
The retina reflects whole-body vascular and inflammatory health. We turn a retinal photo into a non-invasive biomarker platform.

**The problem:**
Lung cancer screening today relies on CT scans — expensive, radiation-based, and underutilized. Most cases are caught late.

**Our solution:**
A 30-second retinal scan using a low-cost fundus camera + deep learning model, deployable in primary care, pharmacies, and community clinics.

**Why it matters:**

**Technology edge:**
